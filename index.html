About Me

For most of his life Omar has been very active, involved in a wide variety of sports such as basketball, soccer, and most recently snowboarding & rock climbing. While he has lived most of his life in South Florida, he spent four years in Tallahassee pursuing his bachelors in marketing. Immediately after college, he obtained his real estate license and has been helping families purchase their forever homes for almost three years now.

While that has brought him many rewarding experiences, he has frequently felt there has been something missing for his own sense of self-fulfillment. He determined that to be his thirst for finding a career that was more mentally challenging & demanding. After much consideration, speaking to many people in the industry, and getting a small taste of web development through Code Academy, where he began learning HTML & JavaScript, he decided that this career path would make the most sense for him and his short-term & long-term goals. He enrolled in the UM coding bootcamp and is excited for what he is to learn and be exposed to in the following 6 months.
